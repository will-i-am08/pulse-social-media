The Process

How It Works

01

Build Your Brand Profiles

Set up each client brand with its own tone, style guidelines, and social handles. Paste a website URL and CaptionCraft's AI will analyse the copy to auto-generate a full brand voice brief.

02

Generate & Approve Content

Upload a photo, select the brand, choose a platform, and hit Generate. Claude AI writes the caption. Your team reviews and marks it approved — or requests a revision in seconds.

03

Publish & Track

Approved posts flow directly to Buffer and onto your social channels via automation. The analytics dashboard tracks publication rates, approvals, and content performance over time.
